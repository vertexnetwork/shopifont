SHOPIFY TYPOGRAPHY KITS
================================================

Each folder is a complete, self-contained kit: a proven font pairing,
copy-paste install code for all 13 free Shopify OS 2.0 themes, a
visual specimen, font-download instructions, a license/usage sheet,
and an uninstall sheet.

KITS IN THIS BUNDLE
  - Premium Editorial/  —  Premium fashion, food, and curated-goods brands
  - Luxury Classic/  —  Beauty, jewelry, and luxury-positioning apparel
  - DTC Friendly/  —  Skincare, supplements, and DTC lifestyle brands
  - Editorial Warm/  —  Editorial brands, artisan food, and long-copy stores
  - Modern Tech/  —  Consumer electronics, smart home, and tech-leaning DTC
  - Minimal & Fast/  —  Minimal, performance-first, single-family storefronts

HOW TO USE ANY KIT
1. Open the kit folder for your store's vibe.
2. Read its README.txt (2 minutes).
3. Get the fonts (fonts/DOWNLOAD.txt — free, from Google Fonts).
4. Find your theme under install/, follow INSTALL.txt.

LICENSE IN ONE LINE
Every font is SIL Open Font License — free for commercial Shopify
use, self-hosted, no tiers. Per-kit LICENSE-AND-USAGE.txt has the
details and the no-questions refund policy.
